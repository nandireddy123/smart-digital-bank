What's inside

Three account types, three logins

Customer

Register, verify by email, open Savings / Current / Student accounts, deposit, withdraw, transfer, apply for loans.

Employee

Approves new accounts and verifies customer details. Created only by a Manager.

Manager

Creates employees, approves loans, freezes/activates accounts, views reports.
